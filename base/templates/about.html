Who we are?

Our Story, Your Pizza

Hello Pizza isn’t just your regular restaurant, no. We’re a family! Nestled in the heart of Blairmore, Alberta, we’ve been serving up smiles. One slice at a time for years. Our passion for food runs deep. It shows in every dish we create. Hand-tossed pizzas? Hearty burgers? Creamy pastas? No matter what dish you order, you’ll notice how we’ve poured our heart into every recipe.
But what truly sets us apart is our love for this community. Hello Pizza is where friends gather, families celebrate, and cravings are met with joy. Put simply, you’ll come for the food, stay for the vibe, and leave as part of our story…
